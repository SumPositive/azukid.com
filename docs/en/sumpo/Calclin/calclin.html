Calclin User Guide

Full-featured calculator app

Tap the keys like a calculator to enter an expression. Tap [=] at the end to get the answer.

That's all for basic operation.

If you're curious, the features below are tucked away — try intuitive gestures to find and use them.

Features

Choose the decimal point symbolDefault: [.] period
Change the number of decimal digits shownDefault: 3 digits, up to 10 digits
Choose a rounding modeDefault: round half to even (banker's rounding)
Choose the digit grouping symbolDefault: [,] comma
Choose the digit grouping styleEvery 3 digits, every 4 digits, Indian style, etc.
Use multiple calculators side by sideUp to triple deck. Use [+][−] header buttons to add or remove panels. Swipe or tap the indicator to switch. Double-tap to toggle 1-panel ⇔ 2-panel view.
History of expressions and answersSwipe left: delete　Swipe right: add memo / copy expression / copy answer　Double-tap: copy expression
Add notes to history entries
Enlarge or reduce text size
Perform unit-aware calculationsLength, area, volume, weight
Lay out the keyboard to your liking
Switch keyboards5 available layouts

Specifications

Calculation Engine

Engine C/C++ fixed-point decimal. Uses 60 of over 2 billion significant digits.
Operations Four arithmetic operations
Calc fields Reverse Polish style, up to 3 fields
History list Up to 100 entries. Oldest entries are deleted first (circular).

Numeric & Display Settings

Decimal digits 0–10 (max 15)
Rounding modes
  • Round up — always toward positive infinity
  • Round toward +∞ — always increases
  • Round half away from zero [JIS Z 8401 Rule B]
  • ★ Round half to even [JIS Z 8401 Rule A] — even rounding / banker's rounding
  • Round half toward +∞
  • Round toward −∞ — always decreases
  • Truncate — always toward zero
Decimal point
  • ★ [.] period
  • [·] middle dot
  • [,] comma
Grouping style
  • No grouping [123456789.0]
  • ★ Every 3 digits [123,456,789.0]
  • Indian style [12,34,56,789.0]
  • Every 4 digits [1,2345,6789.0]
Grouping symbol
  • ★ [,] comma
  • ['] apostrophe
  • [ ] space
  • [.] period

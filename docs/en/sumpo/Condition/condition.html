Condition User Guide

Health monitoring app

Record and manage your health data — blood pressure, heart rate, body temperature, weight, and more.

Measurement timing (wake, post-exercise, etc.) is automatically classified from the time of entry. Track trends with graphs, run statistical analyses, and sync with the Apple Health app.

Features

Record 6 types of health dataBlood pressure, heart rate, temperature, weight, body fat %, skeletal muscle %
Automatic timing classificationWake, rest, pre-sleep, etc. — determined from the time of entry
View trends in graphsSwitch between 1-week to 1-year periods
Statistical analysisBP distribution, JSH guideline ratios, pulse pressure analysis, and more
Sync with Apple HealthChoose write-only, read-only, or bidirectional sync
Set personal goalsTarget values for BP, weight, and other metrics
Export to PDF, CSV, or JSONPrint as PDF · open CSV in Excel · do what you like with JSON
Customize displayed fields and orderConfigure separately for the record form and graphs
Sync settings via iCloudShare your settings across multiple devices

Specifications

Recorded Metrics & Input Ranges

Systolic BP 30–300 mmHg
Diastolic BP 20–200 mmHg
Heart rate 10–200 bpm
Body temperature 31.0–42.9 °C
Body weight 0.0–200.0 kg
Body fat % 0.0–100.0 %
Skeletal muscle % 0.0–100.0 %

Measurement Timing (Auto-classification)

Method Automatically classified from the time of entry. Time-of-day assignments are customizable in Settings.
Categories
  • Wake — immediately upon waking
  • Rest — in a resting state
  • Before sleep — just before going to bed
  • During sleep
  • Pre-exercise — before starting a workout
  • Post-exercise — after finishing a workout
Default time map
  • 0–2h: During sleep
  • 3–7h: Wake
  • 8–10h: Rest
  • 11–13h: Pre-exercise
  • 14–16h: Post-exercise
  • 17–19h: Rest
  • 20–21h: Before sleep
  • 22–23h: During sleep

Graphs

View periods 1 week, 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, 1 year
Graph types
  • Blood pressure (systolic / diastolic)
  • Pulse pressure (systolic − diastolic)
  • Heart rate
  • Body temperature
  • Body weight
  • Weight change
  • Body fat %
  • Skeletal muscle %
  • BMI (requires height setting)
Customization Toggle graph visibility and reorder as preferred

Statistical Analysis

Analysis period 1 week, 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, 1 year
Sections
  • Blood pressure distribution
  • BP / JSH guideline compliance ratio
  • BP / measurement timing correlation
  • 24-hour blood pressure distribution
  • Weight summary statistics
  • Temperature summary
  • 24-hour temperature distribution
  • Temperature frequency distribution
Customization Toggle section visibility and reorder as preferred

Apple Health Integration

Sync direction Write-only, read-only, or bidirectional
Sync timing Automatic (on save / on view) or manual (button)
Supported data Blood pressure, heart rate, body temperature, weight, body fat % (skeletal muscle % not supported)

Item Overview

Tap "Item Overview" in the footer of the group list screen to browse all items across every group in a pack. Perfect for a final check before departure or reviewing your load. Keyword search is also supported.

Sort options

Unchecked Unchecked items appear first. Ideal for a final pre-departure review.
Shortage count Items where stock falls short of required quantity, sorted by largest shortfall first.
Shortage weight Items sorted by the weight of their shortage (shortage count Г— unit weight), heaviest first.
Stock weight Items sorted by current stock weight (stock count Г— unit weight), heaviest first. Useful when reviewing an overweight pack.

Other

Keyword search Filters by item name and memo in real time.
Auto-sort When enabled, the list re-sorts automatically as you check items or update stock.

Quantity Input

Weight per item, stock count, and required quantity can each be entered in two ways.

Input methods

Dial Drag the dial to the right of each field left or right to increase or decrease the value. The dial style (appearance) and sensitivity (pitch, smoothing, inertia) can be customized in Dial Settings.
Numeric keypad Tap the number shown in a field to open a dedicated numeric keypad sheet where you can type a value directly.

Dial Settings

Style Choose from 9 visual styles: Legacy, Midnight, Brass, Ocean, Shape, Varnia, Chrome, Hairline, or Rubber.
Sensitivity preset Five presets available: Fine, Mild, Standard, Light, and Fast.
Fine-tuning Adjust pitch, smoothing, and inertia start velocity individually with sliders.

Pack Sharing & Backup

Export

Single pack Choose "Export" from the pack menu to export one pack as a .packlin file. Share it via AirDrop, email, Notes, or any other method.
All packs (backup) Use "Export All Packs (Backup)" in Settings to save all packs as a single .packlin file. Useful for device migration and data safety.

Import

How to import Tap "Load Pack" in Settings and select a file. Both single-pack and backup files are imported the same way.
Duplicate handling When restoring a backup, packs with matching IDs are overwritten. For single-pack imports, packs with the same name are overwritten. Non-matching packs are added as new.
File detection The app detects whether a file is a single pack or a backup by reading the JSON content, not the file extension.

AI Generation (Chappy)Optional В· In-app purchase

Tell Chappy where you're going or what the occasion is, and the AI will suggest a complete pack for you. The suggested pack is saved directly and can be edited freely afterward. You can also hand an existing pack to Chappy and ask for revisions.

Each generation costs 1 AI ticket. Tickets are available via in-app purchase (1 or 5 tickets) or by watching an ad.

Settings

Display

Display mode Beginner: shows operation hints in the header. Expert: hides hints for a compact layout.
Appearance System: follows the device setting. Light: always light mode. Dark: always dark mode.
Row detail Controls how many lines are shown per row. Minimal: name 1 line, memo hidden. 1 line: name 1 line, memo 1 line. 2 lines: 2 lines each. 3 lines: 3 lines each.

Behavior

New item position Choose whether new items, groups, and packs are added at the top or bottom of the list.

Weight

Show required weight Displays the weight for the required quantity (required Г— unit weight).
Show kg above 1,000 g When the total weight reaches 1,000 g, switches display to "1.0 kg" format.

Check & stock linking

Check ON fills stock When you check an item, the stock quantity is automatically raised to meet the required quantity.
Check OFF clears stock When you uncheck an item, the stock quantity is reset to 0.

Packlin User Guide

Packing checklist app

A packing checklist useful for travel and events. Edit freely as you like.

Use it for trips and business travel. Weight totals help you pack to match your strength. It also works well for managing hobby gear.

About Undo

The goal is to minimize extra steps. There are no confirmation prompts and no Save button after editing. "Delete" removes items immediately without confirmation.

If you make a mistake, tap "Undo ↩︎" to revert to the previous state — up to 10 steps back. Redo lets you step forward again.

Data Structure

Layers Three layers: Pack → Group → Item. Item is the smallest unit.
Pack Bags, backpacks, trunks, storage, etc.
Group Pouches, boxes — subdivisions within a Pack
Item Individual objects

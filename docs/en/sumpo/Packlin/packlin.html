Packlin User Guide

Packing checklist app

A packing checklist useful for travel and events. Edit freely as you like.

Use it for trips and business travel. Weight totals help you pack to match your strength. It also works well for managing hobby gear.

About Undo

The goal is to minimize extra steps. There are no confirmation prompts and no Save button after editing. "Delete" removes items immediately without confirmation.

If you make a mistake, tap "Undo ↩︎" to revert to the previous state — up to 10 steps back. Redo lets you step forward again.

Pack Sharing & Backup

Export

Single pack Choose "Export" from the pack menu to export one pack as a .packlin file. Share it via AirDrop, email, Notes, or any other method.
All packs (backup) Use "Export All Packs (Backup)" in Settings to save all packs as a single .packlin file. Useful for device migration and data safety.

Import

How to import Tap "Load Pack" in Settings and select a file. Both single-pack and backup files are imported the same way.
Duplicate handling When restoring a backup, packs with matching IDs are overwritten. For single-pack imports, packs with the same name are overwritten. Non-matching packs are added as new.
File detection The app detects whether a file is a single pack or a backup by reading the JSON content, not the file extension.

AI Generation (Chappy)Optional · In-app purchase

Tell Chappy where you're going or what the occasion is, and the AI will suggest a complete pack for you. The suggested pack is saved directly and can be edited freely afterward. You can also hand an existing pack to Chappy and ask for revisions.

Each generation costs 1 AI ticket. Tickets are available via in-app purchase (1 or 5 tickets) or by watching an ad.

How to use

Create new Tap the ✦ button in the top-right of the pack list and choose "Ask Chappy to create one."
Revise existing Tap the ✦ button in the pack edit screen and choose "Ask Chappy to revise this." The current pack contents are passed to the AI for additions or edits.
Request text Up to 1,000 characters. Describe the destination, duration, purpose, season, or anything else that helps.

How to get tickets

In-app purchase Available in sets of 1 or 5 tickets.
Watch an ad Tickets can also be earned by watching a video ad from the "Support with an Ad" button in Settings.

Settings

Display

Display mode Beginner: shows operation hints in the header. Expert: hides hints for a compact layout.
Row detail Controls how many lines are shown per row. Minimal: name 1 line, memo hidden. 1 line: name 1 line, memo 1 line. 2 lines: 2 lines each. 3 lines: 3 lines each.

Behavior

New item position Choose whether new items, groups, and packs are added at the top or bottom of the list.

Weight

Show required weight Displays the weight for the required quantity (required × unit weight).
Show kg above 1,000 g When the total weight reaches 1,000 g, switches display to "1.0 kg" format.

Check & stock linking

Check ON fills stock When you check an item, the stock quantity is automatically raised to meet the required quantity.
Check OFF clears stock When you uncheck an item, the stock quantity is reset to 0.

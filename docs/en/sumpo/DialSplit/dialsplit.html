DialSplit User Guide

Dial-style bill splitting

DialSplit helps you split a total amount by adjusting people and amounts with tactile dial controls.

Set the total and panel values, then DialSplit automatically computes Panel A's amount. Amounts follow the currency format of your device locale.

Basic Usage

Set the total amountUse the total dial on the top panel, or tap the amount text to enter with the numpad.
Set people countsUse the left dial on each A/B/C/D panel. Panel A requires at least 1 person.
Set amountsAdjust amounts on B/C/D with the right dial. Panel A amount is auto-calculated.
Change dial stepUse Amount Dial Step to switch the amount dial increment. Step labels are shown as numbers without a currency symbol.
Tune panel styleAdjust panel brightness, text color, and tone from the Panel Style section.
Lock people countUse the lock button on the total panel to freeze people dials and prevent accidental changes.
Lock all controlsUse the lock button on Panel A to freeze every dial at once.
Tune dial sensitivityOpen Dial Settings in Settings to adjust the dial sensitivity to your preference.

Settings

TiersChoose a preset display name set, or edit the display names for A/B/C/D individually.
Dial SettingsAdjust the dial feel, brightness, and response from the settings sheet.
Appearance ModeChoose Auto, Light, or Dark. Auto follows the system setting.
BackgroundChoose Monotone, Brown Leather, or Black Leather.
Amount Dial StepCustomize the five step definitions based on the smallest unit of the current currency.

Specifications

People range A: 1-99, B/C/D: 0-99
Amount range 0-999,900, with currency decimal digits based on locale
Currency display Follows the device locale. Decimal currencies are handled in their smallest currency unit.
Input methods Dial input + direct numpad input
Languages Japanese / English
